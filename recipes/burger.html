Perfect Basic Burger

These burger patties are made with ground beef and an easy bread crumb mixture. Nothing beats a simple hamburger on a warm summer evening. Pile these burgers with your favorite condiments and pop open a cool drink!


    Ingredients
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 poundground beef
  • 1/2 cup fine bread crumbs
    Steps
  1. Preheat grill with high heat and light oil grate
  2. whisk together egg,salt,and pepper in bowl
  3. add ground beef and bread crumbs and mix until well blended
  4. form into 3/4 inch-thick patties
  5. Place patties on the preheated grill. Cover and cook 6 to 8 minutes per side, or to desired doneness. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C)
